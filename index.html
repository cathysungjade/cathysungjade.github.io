Faroe Island

March 2018

Farao Island is one of the most beautiful places I have ever been! If you have a chance travelling to the small island is one thing I recommend. During the trip, seeing the beautiful landscape, my passion for photography sparked. Later on, after looking though all the photos I took, I was inspired to write a short prose "A Park" highlighting the different experience between our modern society in Toronto and Faroe Island. Olive Trees

April 2020

Being quarantined at home made me realize what I took for granted. It reminded me of times when I was able to travel and see different parts of the world. In particular, it reminded me of the late night strolls with friends in Italy and seeing the beautiful Olive Trees in real life. I decided to paint a garden of Olive Trees, a place I would rather be than at home.

  • Category: Painting

Sunflowers

December 2019

Vivid memories of my childhood that I carry with me is gardening with my grandpa. My grandpa had a green thumb, meaning he had a gift for growing plants. While I did not help with the gardening because I was scared of bugs, I remember watching him while laying in the sun. My grandpa loved gardening and in particular loved Sunflowers. While he stopped gardening because of his age, I decided to paint him Sunflowers, to remind him of the time we spent together when I was younger.

  • Date: December 2019
  • Category: Painting
